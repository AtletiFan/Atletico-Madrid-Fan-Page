Atlético Madrid: Simeone’s Arrival & La Liga Glory (2011–2014)

After years of inconsistency, **Diego Simeone** took over as **Atlético Madrid manager in December 2011**, bringing a new era of discipline, passion, and defensive solidity.

Building a New Identity (2011–2013)

  • Under **Simeone**, Atlético quickly improved, winning the **2011–12 UEFA Europa League**. 🏆
  • In **2013**, they defeated Real Madrid **2-1 in the Copa del Rey final**, breaking a **14-year winless streak against their rivals**. ⚔️

The Legendary 2013–14 La Liga Campaign

Against all odds, Atlético Madrid **won La Liga in 2013–14**, becoming the first team outside Barcelona & Real Madrid to do so since 2004.

The title was secured on the final day **against Barcelona at Camp Nou (1-1)**, with **Diego Godín scoring the decisive header**. 🏆
