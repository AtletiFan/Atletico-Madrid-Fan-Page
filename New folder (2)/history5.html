Atlético Madrid: The Transition Years (1987–2005)

The period between **1987 and 2005** was one of major ups and downs for Atlético Madrid. It saw the club go from **domestic success to relegation**, followed by a slow but steady return to the top.

Jesús Gil Era & La Liga Double (1987–1996)

Jesús Gil took over as club president in **1987**, bringing financial backing but also controversy. After several managerial changes, **Radomir Antić** led Atlético to a historic **domestic double** in **1995–96**, winning **La Liga** and the **Copa del Rey**.

Relegation & Second Division Years (1999–2002)

  • 1999 - Club shockingly **relegated** from La Liga.
  • 2000–2002 - Spent **two seasons in Segunda División** before returning.

Return to La Liga & Slow Rebuild (2002–2005)

After returning to La Liga in **2002**, Atlético struggled to regain its former glory. However, the arrival of **Fernando Torres** as a star striker gave fans hope for the future.
