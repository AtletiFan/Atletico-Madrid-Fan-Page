Champions League Final Heartbreaks (2014 & 2016)

Atlético Madrid came agonizingly close to European glory **twice** under Diego Simeone but suffered **heartbreaking losses** to Real Madrid in both finals.

2014 Final: Lisbon Disaster

  • Atlético Madrid faced **Real Madrid** in the **2014 UEFA Champions League final**.
  • Diego Godín’s **first-half goal** gave Atlético the lead, and they defended resolutely.
  • In the **93rd minute**, **Sergio Ramos equalized**, forcing extra time.
  • Real Madrid eventually won **4-1**, leaving Atlético devastated.

2016 Final: Agonizing Penalty Shootout

Two years later, Atlético met **Real Madrid again** in the final, this time in Milan.

Atlético **conceded first** but equalized through **Yannick Carrasco**.

Antoine Griezmann had missed a crucial **penalty during normal time**, and the game ended **1-1** after extra time.

In the **penalty shootout**, **Juanfran hit the post**, and **Cristiano Ronaldo scored the winning penalty**, giving Real Madrid another victory.
