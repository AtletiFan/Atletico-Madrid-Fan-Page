Atlético Madrid: European Cup Finalists (1965–1974)

The period between **1965 and 1974** saw Atlético Madrid establish itself as one of Europe's top clubs, consistently competing at the highest level and reaching the **European Cup final** in 1974.

Key Achievements (1965–1974)

  • 1966 - Won **Copa del Generalísimo** (Copa del Rey).
  • 1970 - Finished **La Liga runners-up**, securing European qualification.
  • 1972 - Won another **Copa del Rey** title.
  • 1973 - **La Liga Champions** under **Max Merkel**.
  • 1974 - Reached the **European Cup Final**, narrowly losing to Bayern Munich.

1974 European Cup Final

Atlético Madrid faced **Bayern Munich** in the **1974 European Cup Final** at Heysel Stadium. The match ended **1-1** after a last-minute equalizer by Bayern’s **Hans-Georg Schwarzenbeck**. In the replay two days later, Bayern dominated and won **4-0**, denying Atlético their first European Cup.
